Lawyer Up, Level Up: Where Clients and Lawyers Team Up!

PROJECT TYPE

Service Innovation & Web UI Design & Legal Design

PROJECT INTRODUCTION

Within conventional legal services, attorneys hold positions of authority, while clients typically find themselves in subordinate roles. This hierarchical structure frequently results in feelings of helplessness and discontentment among clients, impeding the effective delivery of services.

I revamped the legal service process for civil cases. Initially, I focused on enhancing the physical environment of the law firm to facilitate seamless communication between clients and lawyers. Additionally, I devised a collaborative case analysis website, developed in partnership with lawyers and clients, complemented by paper-based case analysis forms.

By implementing the design above approach, it is possible to enhance the efficacy of communication between lawyers and clients while promoting greater client engagement. This, in turn, ensures that clients are well-informed about the case process and facilitates the resolution of issues within the current legal service framework.
