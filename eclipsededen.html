ECLIPSED EDEN

From Seed to Price: The Cost of Being Wanted

PROJECT TYPE

Multimedia Interactive & Art Installation

PROJECT INTRODUCTION

We focus on the phenomenon of assimilation in today's commodified society, from the social fact that the primitive nature of the wilderness is exploited and transformed by capital into a 5A scenic spot.

Therefore, we associate plants with the primitive seeds planted and fertilized by human beings, and transformed into priced flowers or potted plants.

At the same time, this is a metaphor for the development process of human beings from ignorance to being instilled with all kinds of knowledge, and finally being assimilated into the commoditized society, thus generating self-exploitation, just to achieve the fact that they can become “commodities” with “high price & competed for”.
