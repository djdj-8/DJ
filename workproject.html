PROJECT INTRODUCTION

In preparation for the June 2024 launch of NIO’s “Driving Enjoyment” (驾享) scenario-based services, I was involved in improving the service flow and product functions.

The existing process had several pain points for both users and specialists. I helped organize a 5-day design sprint, using tools like Xmind to clarify goals and collect over 50 user pain points.

I also conducted research on travel trends and competitive services to inform our design. Additionally, I streamlined the service standards, reducing complexity and improving the training process for over 6000 B-end users, ensuring a smoother service experience for the upcoming launch.
