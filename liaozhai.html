聊斋·目欲

Desire Meets Power: Reimagining Gender Through Timeless Tales.

PROJECT TYPE

Creative Video Design & 3D Design

PROJECT INTRODUCTION

Liaozhai Zhiyi is a collection of short stories from ancient China. This project considers gender relations in some of these stories: imaging is the main mechanism that fuels the imagination of desire.

The men in the book stay at the level of "desire", while the women are mostly symbols of sexuality. Here the two sexes are in control of each other: the woman with her "desire" and the man with his "power".

This builds the worldview of the whole project.
