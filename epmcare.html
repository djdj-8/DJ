EPM - Care

For Paediatric Epilepsy.

PROJECT TYPE

Interaction Design & UI Design & Product Design

PROJECT INTRODUCTION

Inspired by a documentary about the difficulties of a paediatric epilepsy patient and the pain and helplessness suffered by his family, I am motivated to leverage my design thinking skills to offer assistance to this vulnerable group and contribute to the advancement of rehabilitation treatment.

I've developed a range of music therapy products along with accompanying apps. These products allow patients to attach patches to their limbs, engage in music games, and simulate playing musical instruments using a controller. The patches generate low-frequency vibrations synchronized with the music, providing pain relief. By participating in music games, patients can also exercise their limbs and brain, reducing the risk of complications associated with pediatric epilepsy.
