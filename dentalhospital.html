Renovation of Hangzhou Dental Hospital

Comfort in Every Visit: Redesigning Care, Reimagining Fear.

PROJECT TYPE

Service Redesign & UX design

PROJECT INTRODUCTION

I grew up harbouring a fear of dental hospitals. So one day, when I stepped into a dental hospital, I wondered if I could redesign it. Through site visits and extensive research, we identified various issues and implemented innovative changes to create a patient-centric environment that enhances the overall experience. Based on my research, I have discovered that individuals have shifted away from finding fulfilment in the excessive expenses associated with material consumption in recent years. Additionally, we developed a unique logo and accompanying signage to alleviate patients' apprehensions and fears related to dental visits. We aimed to enhance efficiency and overall patient experience by incorporating these elements and other related improvements.
